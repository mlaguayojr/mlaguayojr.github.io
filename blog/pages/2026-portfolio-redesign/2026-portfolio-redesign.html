Five tabs, each answering a different question you might have:

  • About — who I am and what I know.
  • Blog — things I've built, broken, or figured out along the way.
  • Career — professional work and what I shipped on the job.
  • Education — my academic background.
  • Projects — personal experiments and side builds.

The look and feel was inspired by the Jekyll Minimalist theme — clean layout, sidebar navigation, and a focus on readability over flash. It felt like the right starting point for a portfolio that's meant to be read, not just glanced at.
